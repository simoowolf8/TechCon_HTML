History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup for tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a major annual event attracting thousands of attendees from around the world.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation and collaboration in the tech industry. We aim to provide a platform for sharing knowledge, networking, and showcasing the latest advancements in technology.

Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Known as the first computer programmer, Dr. Ada Lovelace's work laid the foundation for modern computing.

Mr. Alan Turing

A pioneer in computer science, Mr. Alan Turing's contributions have been instrumental in the development of artificial intelligence.

Ms. Grace Hopper

Ms. Grace Hopper was a trailblazer in programming and invented one of the first compilers, making programming languages more accessible.
